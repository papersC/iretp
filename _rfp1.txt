1. Introduction 
(To be completed by the requesting department) 
The Dubai Land Department (DLD) hereby issues this Request for Proposal (RFP) for the design, 
development, deployment, maintenance, and post -go-live support of the Integrated Real Estate 
Transparency Platform (IRETP). The primary strategic objective is to advance Dubai's standing in the JLL 
Global Real Estate Transparency Index (GRETI) and to reinforce investor confidence among international 
financial institutions, sovereign wealth funds, and global investors. 
The platform directly supports the Dubai Economic Agenda D33 and the Smart Dubai Strategy by providing 
open, accurate, real-time, and interactive real estate data to the public while equipping DLD leadership with 
powerful internal risk management and developer oversight tools. 
General Introduction 
Dubai Land Department is a government entity falls under the umbrella of the Dubai Executive Council 
responsible for registering, regulating properties, promoting real estate investment and settlement of rent 
disputes. In addition, the dept. is responsible to educate and raise the awareness of real estate stakeholders.  
Dubai Land Department provides a comprehensive range of real estate services, most notably the 
responsibility of overlooking sales transactions, regulations, and registration of all transactions in real estate 
market. Playing a significant role in the necessary improvement processes to accelerate DubaiΓÇÖs real estate 
sector.

2. Scope of Work  
 
The selected vendor shall design, develop, integrate, and deploy an end-to-end Integrated Real Estate 
Transparency Platform for Dubai Land Department, comprising a public investor-facing portal and an 
internal management system. The scope includes delivering real-time data visualization, GIS mapping, self-
service analytics, open data APIs, and an AI-powered data assistant (non-advisory), alongside internal 
capabilities such as an Early Warning Risk System, escrow monitoring, and a developer performance rating 
engine. The vendor shall ensure full integration with DLDΓÇÖs existing data ecosystem, maintain high data 
accuracy and governance standards, implement a secure and scalable architecture compliant with UAE 
regulations, and deliver the solution in phased releases within the defined timeline, followed by post-go-live 
warranty and technical support.
